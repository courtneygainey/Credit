How to Build Good Credit.

Building a great credit score is a vital step in achieving financial security. A good score can help you attain important life and financial milestones because it is a determinant in receiving mortagages, loans, and apartment leases. By committing to making timely payments on bills or debt, you're already on the path to building a stellar score.

Here are some steps for building your score:

  • Use a starter credit card if you are building credit history from scratch.
  • Make it a point to pay all of your bills on time.
  • Make regular payments on your student loans.
  • Take out car loans or other personal loans to build credit history.
